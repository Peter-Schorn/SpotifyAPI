  • images

    The Images for the playlist.

    The array may be empty or contain up to three images. The images are returned by size in descending order. See Working with Playlists.

    The dimensions of the images may be nil, especially if uploaded by the user.

    Warning

    The urls of these images, if returned, are temporary and will expire in less than a day. Use SpotifyAPI.playlistImage(_:) to retrieve the image for a playlist.
